WHO WE ARE

Our Identity and Vision

We are Taking Small Steps to Make our community individuals self-Sustainable

Beatrice Hope Foundation

We are dedicated to improve the Livelihoods of the youth, especially the young women, and the under priviledged, enabling them to be self-reliant and productive through project based activities, skill impartation, training and education..

Beatrice Hope Foundation has implemented major developments in the local community including; A skill based training Centre, a community market, an institution of Higher learning, and a food processing mill that benefits the local community.
